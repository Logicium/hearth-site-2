# Hearth — Photo Guide

Hearth is the **Stay** archetype in the Apotome website program.

Drop final, web-optimized JPGs into `public/photos/` using the **exact filenames** below.

| Portfolio    | ~18          | 12            |

## Photo specs

- 2400 px on the long edge minimum
- Color-graded JPG, sRGB, quality ~80
- Beds **made tightly**; remove personal items
- Curtains open, lamps on, no clutter on nightstands

## Tips

- Shoot rooms with a wide lens (24–28mm) at corner angles
- Light interiors with **two windows + one warm lamp** if possible
- Capture **textures**: brick, brass, wood, linen
- Avoid HDR — soft, true-to-life is the Hearth aesthetic
